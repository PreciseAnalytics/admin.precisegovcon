# Interactive Dashboard Guide

## Overview

The admin dashboard now features fully interactive stat cards and metrics across all pages with detailed drill-down modals and helpful explanations. Every stat card is clickable, selectable, and provides deeper insights.

---

## Users Dashboard

### Stat Cards (Interactive)

#### 1. **Total Users Card**
- Shows total number of registered users
- **Click to View:** Tier distribution breakdown
  - Enterprise tier count
  - Professional tier count
  - Basic tier count
  - Free tier count
- **Filter Feature:** Click any tier in modal to filter user list by that tier

#### 2. **Active Users Card**
- Shows number of users with active subscriptions
- **Click to View:** Status distribution breakdown
  - Active users
  - Trialing users
  - Pending users
  - Cancelled users
- **Filter Feature:** Click any status to filter user list

#### 3. **Suspended Users Card**
- Shows number of suspended accounts
- **Click to View:** Suspension details and management options

#### 4. **Paid Subscribers Card**
- Shows number of users with paid subscriptions
- **Click to View:** Revenue metrics and insights

### Features
- ✅ Hover effects with scale transform (105%)
- ✅ Color-coded border changes on hover
- ✅ "Click to see details" helper text
- ✅ Smooth drill-down modals with progress bars
- ✅ Percentage breakdowns in modals
- ✅ Direct filtering from modal selections

## Subscriptions Dashboard

### Stat Cards (Interactive)

#### 1. **Total Subscriptions Card**
- Shows total active subscriptions
- **Click to View:** Tier breakdown modal
  - Enterprise (Purple)
  - Professional (Blue)
  - Basic (Green)
  - Trial (Orange)
- **Filter Feature:** Click tier to filter subscription list

#### 2. **MRR Card** (Monthly Recurring Revenue)
- Shows total monthly recurring revenue
- **Full Name:** Monthly Recurring Revenue
- **Meaning:** Total predictable monthly revenue from active subscriptions
- **Click to View:** Current MRR breakdown

#### 3. **ARR Card** (Annual Recurring Revenue)
- Shows total annual recurring revenue
- **Full Name:** Annual Recurring Revenue
- **Meaning:** Total predictable annual revenue (MRR × 12)
- **Calculation:** MRR multiplied by 12 months
- **Click to View:** Current ARR breakdown

#### 4. **ARPU Card** (Average Revenue Per User)
- Shows average revenue per user
- **Full Name:** Average Revenue Per User
- **Meaning:** Average revenue generated by each paying subscriber
- **Formula:** Total Revenue ÷ Number of Paying Users
- **Importance:** Key metric for pricing strategy and profitability
- **Click to View:** Per-tier ARPU breakdown

### Abbreviations Explained

| Abbreviation | Full Name | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| **MRR** | Monthly Recurring Revenue | Total predictable monthly revenue from subscriptions |
| **ARR** | Annual Recurring Revenue | Total predictable annual revenue (MRR × 12) |
| **ARPU** | Average Revenue Per User | Average revenue per paying subscriber |

### Tier Breakdown Cards
Four additional cards show subscription counts by tier:
- **Enterprise** (Purple) - High-value customers
- **Professional** (Blue) - Mid-tier customers
- **Basic** (Green) - Entry-level customers
- **Trial** (Orange) - Trial users

Each card has a "View All" button to filter subscriptions by that tier.

### Features
- ✅ All cards have hover animations
- ✅ Helper text explaining each metric
- ✅ Interactive drill-down modals
- ✅ Tier filtering from modals
- ✅ Full metric explanations in parentheses

### Key Insights Section (Interactive)

#### Conversion Rate (Free to Paid)
- **Full Name:** Conversion Rate
- **Definition:** Percentage of all users who converted to paid subscriptions
- **Formula:** (Active Subscriptions ÷ Total Users) × 100
- **Importance:** Measures sales effectiveness
- **Click to View:** Detailed conversion metrics
- **In Parentheses:** "(Free to Paid)"

#### ARPU (Average Revenue Per User)
- **Full Name:** Average Revenue Per User
- **Definition:** Average revenue per paying subscriber
- **Formula:** Total Revenue ÷ Active Subscriptions
- **Importance:** Indicates pricing strategy effectiveness
- **Click to View:** Detailed revenue per user breakdown
- **In Parentheses:** "(Avg Revenue Per User)"

#### Engagement Rate (Daily Active)
- **Full Name:** Engagement Rate
- **Definition:** Percentage of total users active in the last 24 hours
- **Formula:** (Active Today ÷ Total Users) × 100
- **Importance:** Measures user satisfaction and platform usage
- **Click to View:** Detailed engagement metrics
- **In Parentheses:** "(Daily Active)"
